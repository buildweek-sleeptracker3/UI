Meet the Team

These are the developers behind the Sleep Tracker app. You can find all of us on LinkedIn if you'd like to reach out to learn more or to chat about our experiences working on this project.

Barbara Moore

UI Developer -

Barbara created the landing, about us, and features pages on the marketing site. Her favorite thing about coding is being able to make a site look just like her idea or a design sketch. In her free time, Barbara enjoys playing video games and doing jigsaw puzzles.

Brian Russell

Front End React Developer -

Brian added all of the routing for the application, as well as built the login and signup pages. His favorite thing about coding is the way it feels like you're solving a puzzle. When he has spare time, Brian enjoys watching too many movies, playing guitar, and cooking.

Victoria Topham

Front End React Developer -

Victoria handled connecting the front and back ends, and also managed state throughout the application. Her favorite thing (or least favorite thing, maybe) about coding is spending 45 minutes debugging, only to realize it was a misplaced comma all along. She likes to cook in her free time, and enjoys gaming.

Cody Denniston

Backend Developer -

Cody assisted with the creation of the API endpoints, formatted the data, and added authorization to the application. His favorite thing about coding is the moment when all the effort comes together into a functional app. He enjoys running and playing video games in his spare time.

Henry Ramirez

Backend Developer -

Henry created the API endpoints and tests, as well as developed the authorization and data schema for Sleep Tracker. His favorite thing about coding is seeing the successful result at the end of all the hard work. Henry also runs a landscaping business on the side and enjoys trying out new things in his free time.
