Why Sleep Tracker?

What sets Sleep Tracker apart from the hundreds of other apps out there that record your sleep? Here are some key features that will help you see why Sleep Tracker is a cut above the rest.

Track Your Sleep

Sleep Tracker provides (as you would expect) the ability to enter in the hours you slept during the night. While this isn't new technology by any means, it's clearly the most important feature!

You'll enter this information by selecting the time you went to bed and the time you got up on a scroll wheel. The app will then calculate exactly how long you slept and store it in an entry for that day.
